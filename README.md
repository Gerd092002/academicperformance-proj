For All Users: Start by visiting the website and logging in with your username and password. 
Once logged in, you’ll see a dashboard made just for your role. When you're finished, 
always click Logout in the top corner to keep your account secure. 

If You’re a Teacher: Use your dashboard to create assignments, grade student work, and 
manage your classes. Click "My Classes" to view students, then select an assignment to 
enter grades and feedback. To add new assignments, go to "Create Content," fill in the 
details, and click Publish. You can also add students to your subjects through "Enroll 
Students." 

If You’re a Student: Your dashboard shows upcoming assignments and your current grades. 
To submit work, go to "My Assignments," find the task, upload your file, and click Submit. To 
check your progress, click "My Grades" to view scores, averages, and charts for each 
subject. 

If You’re an Admin: You can add and remove users and subjects from the "Manage 
Accounts" and "Manage Subjects" sections. To create a new account, fill in the user’s 
details, select their role, and click Create Account. To add a subject, enter its name, assign 
a teacher, and save.
